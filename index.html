Founded in 2010 in Oklahoma City, Little Lemon has expanded its global reach, sharing its delectable flavors with food enthusiasts worldwide. Building upon our strong reputation in Japan, we aim to replicate our success and deliver exceptional dining experiences in every location we serve. While many people appreciate Indian cuisine, many are unaware of or dislike the common unhealthy ingredients that contribute to the rich flavor of traditional Indian dishes.
